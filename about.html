Mision

To provide the Dominican Republic and the Caribbean region with a carrier operating alongside the giants of Air Transportion. to provide Dominican travelers with the possibility of choosing their national flag carrier and enjoy a service like being at home, with preferences to enhance national pride and serve their vacation needs. To help develop the air transport market in Dominican republic as local investor providing the structures for the support of all maintenance requirements to be met to comply with the ICAO rules .

To create a business culture to make customer feel like being taken the best care of. And . Jesus Antonio Dinzey Genao

I am Jesus Dinzey, professional Aircraft Mechanic, . I graduated at The School of Aviation in the Dominican Republic in 1987. Hired by American Airlines as a ground support/ line mechanic. In 1992 employed by the defunct airline Dominicana de Aviacion , which subsequently closed, went to USA in 1993 to validate my license within USA, returned to Dominican Republic as a full rated A&P mechanic.

In 1997 was hired to be the TAP Air Portugal mechanic, and returned as a fully qualified mechanic rated to work in Europe with an ICAO license which was changed in 2006 with the European adoption of the EASA rule to qualify and certify the technical staff.

I have broad experience in part 145 company certification: PAWA DOMINICANA, PAN AM, TAINO AIRLINES, DOMINICAN WINGS; APA AIRWAYS, all of which I assisted in certifying in the DR.
